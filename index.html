Welcome!

My name is Elle, a Detroit-based developer who loves creating software to build great products. My skill set includes: Java, MySQL, Bootstrap 3 and Project Management. I have an appreciation for clean code, progressive software development teams and Agile methodology.

After graduating from University of Michigan and spending a few years working in the nonprofit sector, I was ready to advance my career. I spent almost 2 years working in Project Management and Database Administration with a local nonprofit, but was unfortunately laid off. I sought other opportunities to expand my tech knowledge base, which eventually led me to Grand Circus Detroit. Eight weeks of an intensive back-end bootcamp left me with lifelong connections and a hunger for more.

Graduating in October 2016, I continue to work on freelance projects while seeking entry-level employment with a metro Detroit company. I am passionate about the Latin language and drone piloting. I'm committed to continue learning additional programming languages and finding my niche in Detroit's burgeoning tech scene.
